# Product

A personal developer portfolio website for Ranjan Prasad, an Angular developer. The site showcases skills, projects, and provides a contact form for potential employers or collaborators.

## Pages

- **Home** — Hero section (name, bio, links, CV download) + Skills grid
- **Projects** — Project cards listing personal and featured work
- **Contact** — Reactive form for reaching out

## Purpose

Static/client-side portfolio. No backend — data is hardcoded in Angular services. The contact form currently logs to console (no email integration yet).
